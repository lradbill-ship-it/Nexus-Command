## ✅ Shipped in Session 7 (newest first)
- **🐛 FIX: units permanently stranded by pathfinding‑budget STARVATION** (`<deploy>`, live) — **Lane's 2nd playtest report** ("new harvesters of all types sit outside the war factory, don't go to the rally, don't auto‑harvest, can't be commanded — just stuck"). The earlier re‑path fix wasn't enough: the deeper cause is **budget starvation**. Units are stepped in `game.units` array order; a large active army consumes the entire `resetPathBudget` (60000 pops/tick) *before* later‑processed units are reached, so a fresh unit's `setPath` **always** defers → `waitPath` holds forever. And because `waitPath` short‑circuits `followPath` before stuck‑detection, the harvester's tunnel‑escape never fires either. Re‑pathing every tick doesn't help — every retry re‑defers. Fresh economy units by the factory are hit hardest (appended last in the array). **Fix:** a persistent `waitPath` now escalates to a **FORCED, un‑budgeted `findPath`** after ~1.2s of starvation (`findPath` gained a `force` param that bypasses the budget check; new `Unit.waitSince` tracks the hold). General across ALL unit types — nothing can hang forever. Repro'd headlessly under 70 constantly‑moving units: harvester stuck at factory (`order=move`, `waitPath`, 65px) → now leaves + harvests (`order=idle`, `hState=go`, 218px). tsc + bundle clean, artifact boots no errors. **LESSON: a per‑tick pathfinding budget must have a starvation‑escape valve — array‑order processing means late units can be perpetually starved by an active army; the deferral‑hold from S6 assumed the budget frees within a few ticks, which a big battle violates.**
- **🐛 FIX: harvesters/loggers ignored move clicks under a busy path budget** (`<deploy>`, live) — **Lane's playtest bug** ("when I manufacture harvesters they just won't move once they come out of the factory, no matter where I click"). Root cause: the **harvester & logger move‑order branches in `updateUnit` followed their path but NEVER re‑requested one** — the only two movers the S6 "every move order must re‑path" fix missed. When the per‑tick pathfinding budget is **saturated** (busy mid‑game, many units/economy scans), a manual move's `setPath` defers to `waitPath` with a null/stale path, so the unit holds forever or trails its **old harvest path** instead of going where you clicked. Fix: both branches now re‑path on `waitPath` / consumed‑or‑empty path / a 0.8s timer (matching the combat/Aegis/Envoy movers) and clear `waitPath` on arrival. Repro'd headlessly via a **0‑budget tick** at click time → harvester ended 690px from a 320px‑away target (stuck) → now reaches it (51px, order idle). tsc + bundle clean, artifact boots no errors. **LESSON (extends the S6 path rule): EVERY move branch — incl. the economy units' own dispatch — must re‑path; a budget‑deferred `waitPath` with no retry = a permanently stuck unit.**
